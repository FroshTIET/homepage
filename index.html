About us

The primary task of Frosh is to ensure a hassle-free admission process for Freshers. The transition from school to college can be rough, but that's where Frosh comes in. Through a fun-packed series of electrifying events, interactive games, guest lectures, and cultural and technical workshops, it gives a kick start to the students’ social life. It also provides a platform for students to showcase their talents and emerge as confident individuals. This year, Frosh is prepared to break all preconceived notions about online events. The PROEM week features enthralling enigmas that take you on adventurous journeys from the safety of your homes, and engaging interactions with your peers that you thought you would miss out on. So, are you ready for this once in a lifetime experience?

Welcome aboard the Journey freshers. The journey to explore and experience the new you. To Discover your Inner Self through us.

Our Team!

Selected from different branches, with different talents and personalities, our team is an eclectic group of the best kind. Chosen by the Core after a tough round of interviews, the Mentors form a versatile and multifaceted team. Guided by experienced Core members, the Mentors aid new entrants to settle in the campus environment and thereby ease into their college life. Teachers form our backbone, offering invaluable wisdom and knowledge, thus creating the leaders of tomorrow. With the experience of seniors and the fresh minds of juniors, the team becomes a unit, a family that teaches and learns something new every single day. Together, the team functions and evolves, building each other and working towards becoming the best version of themselves.
